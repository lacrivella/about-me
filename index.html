Basics

Leigh-Ann is originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. She attended the Ohio State University (go bucks!), and recieved a BFA in Glass Construction. After graduation, Leigh-Ann excavated stained glass from a medival archeology site in Ireland. In 2015, Leigh-Ann relocated to the PNW for a job oppurtunity at Pilchuck Glass School. After spending several summers making glass art in the woods, Leigh-Ann decided to shift her focus in a career in software development.
